Mughlai Biryani : Mughlai Biryani uses basmati rice, meat (usually chicken or mutton), yoghurt, cream, nuts (such as almonds and cashews), dried fruits (like raisins), and a variety of aromatic spices including saffron, mace, and nutmeg. The preparation involves marinating the meat in yoghurt and spices, then cooking it until tender.
